I’m Ruby Louro. I am a New England based designer currently pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at the University of Massachusetts in Dartmouth, MA. While I am majoring in Graphic Design at UMass Dartmouth, I am also pursuing a minor in Fashion Design, I love anything that requires creativity and design.

My specialties are in branding identities, multi media design, UX/UI, and fashion. I consider myself an interdisciplinary artist. I may be pursuing a degree in Graphic Design, but I truly feel that as an artist, it is important to explore different mediums and techniques. I greatly enjoy working with small businesses and working with others that genuinely enjoy what they do. If I’m not designing, you will most likely find me curled up reading a good book, watching old films, or rummaging through vintage stores!
